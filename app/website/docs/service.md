1. Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Focuses on restoring movement, strength, and function in the muscles, bones, joints, tendons, and ligaments.

Conditions treated:

* Osteoarthritis
* Rheumatoid Arthritis
* Fractures
* Tendon injuries (e.g., Achilles tendonitis)
* Ligament injuries (e.g., ankle sprains)
* Frozen shoulder
* Chronic neck and back pain

2. Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Designed to help athletes and active individuals recover from injuries and safely return to sport.

Conditions treated:

* Anterior cruciate ligament (ACL) injuries
* Hamstring strains
* Groin strains
* Tennis elbow
* Shin splints
* Shoulder instability
* Muscle tears

3. Pre- and Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Conditions and procedures:

* Joint replacement surgery
* Ligament reconstruction
* Spinal surgery
* Fracture fixation
* Shoulder surgery

4. Chronic Pain Management

Helps individuals manage long-term pain and improve quality of life.

Conditions treated:

* Fibromyalgia
* Chronic low back pain
* Chronic neck pain
* Persistent post-surgical pain
* Arthritis-related pain

5. Neurological Rehabilitation

Supports people with disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.

Conditions treated:

* Stroke
* Parkinson’s Disease
* Multiple Sclerosis
* Spinal cord injury
* Traumatic brain injury
* Peripheral nerve injuries

6. Cardiac Rehabilitation

Improves cardiovascular health and physical function after heart-related conditions.

Conditions treated:

* Coronary Artery Disease
* Heart attack recovery
* Heart failure
* Post-cardiac surgery recovery

7. Women’s Health and Pelvic Rehabilitation

Addresses conditions related to pregnancy, postpartum recovery, and pelvic floor dysfunction.

Conditions treated:

* Fecal and Urinary incontinence
* Pelvic organ prolapse
* Pregnancy-related back and pelvic pain
* Postpartum recovery issues
* Pelvic pain

8. Paediatric Care

Provides physiotherapy services for infants, children, and adolescents.

Conditions treated:

* Cerebral Palsy
* Developmental delays
* Muscular weakness
* Genetic disorders affecting movement
* Sports injuries in children

9. Tele-Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy services delivered remotely through video consultations and digital platforms.

Suitable for:

* Musculoskeletal pain
* Post-operative follow-up
* Exercise supervision
* Chronic condition management

10. Home and Community Physiotherapy Services

Physiotherapy delivered in patients’ homes, workplaces, schools, or community settings.

Conditions treated:

* Mobility limitations
* Post-stroke recovery
* Elderly care needs
* Post-surgical rehabilitation

11. Ergonomics and Workplace Assessment

Focuses on preventing and managing work-related injuries through workplace modifications and education.

Conditions treated:

* Repetitive strain injuries
* Work-related neck pain
* Work-related low back pain
* Poor posture syndromes

12. Geriatric Care Physiotherapy

Specialized physiotherapy for older adults to maintain independence and mobility.

Conditions treated:

* Osteoporosis
* Osteoarthritis
* Balance disorders
* General age-related weakness
* Fall risk

13. Oncology Physiotherapy

Supports individuals undergoing or recovering from cancer treatment.

Conditions treated:

* Cancer-related fatigue
* Post-surgical weakness
* Lymphedema
* Reduced mobility following treatment

14. Vestibular Rehabilitation

Specialized treatment for dizziness, vertigo, and balance disorders related to the inner ear and vestibular system.

Conditions treated:

* Benign Paroxysmal Positional Vertigo
* Vestibular neuritis
* Labyrinthitis
* Chronic dizziness
* Balance impairments
