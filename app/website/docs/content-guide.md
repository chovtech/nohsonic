# Website Content Guide

## Tone

- Simple
- Human
- Non-technical
- Reassuring

---

## Writing Rules

- Avoid medical jargon unless explained
- Focus on outcomes, not procedures
- Use “you” language
- Emphasize recovery and movement

---

## Example Style

Bad:
“Lumbar musculoskeletal dysfunction therapy protocol”

Good:
“Treatment for lower back pain and stiffness”
