Paediatric Care

Physiotherapy services for infants, children, and adolescents.

Children's bodies are different — and their physiotherapy should be too. We work with infants, children, and teenagers using age-appropriate techniques, play-based therapy where possible, and close involvement of parents and caregivers. Early physiotherapy intervention can make a profound difference to a child's development, confidence, and long-term mobility.

Child-Centred, Family-Informed

We assess each child holistically — looking at developmental milestones, movement quality, strength, and coordination. Treatment sessions are structured around the child's attention span and engagement level. We involve parents in every step so that therapy can continue at home, maximising the benefit of each session.

  • Cerebral Palsy
  • Developmental delays
  • Muscular weakness and hypotonia
  • Genetic disorders affecting movement
  • Sports injuries in children and adolescents
  • Scoliosis and postural issues
