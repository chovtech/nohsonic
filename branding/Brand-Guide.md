# NOHSONIC Physiotherapy Clinic — Brand Guide (Source of Truth)

## 1. Brand Identity

NOHSONIC is a modern physiotherapy clinic focused on:
- Pain relief
- Movement restoration
- Spine and musculoskeletal health
- Long-term physical recovery

The brand represents:
- Precision healthcare
- Trust and professionalism
- Clinical but modern experience
- Patient-centered recovery

---

## 2. Brand Personality

- Professional but human
- Calm and reassuring
- Clear and simple communication
- Medical-grade trust with modern simplicity

---

## 3. Visual Identity System

### Core Symbol
Circular spine-based physiotherapy mark with:
- Spine alignment concept
- Target/pain-point emphasis circles
- Movement & correction symbolism

---

## 4. Color System

Primary Blue: #0F4C97  
Light Blue: #7CB4E8  
Accent Blue: #2E86DE  
White: #FFFFFF  

Usage:
- Primary Blue → trust, headers, core UI
- Light Blue → backgrounds, soft surfaces
- Accent Blue → highlights, actions, focus points

---

## 5. Typography

Headings: Montserrat  
Body: Open Sans  

Rules:
- Headings = structured, clinical clarity
- Body = readable, calm, non-clinical tone

---

## 6. UI Tone (Digital Products)

- Clean spacing
- High readability
- Low cognitive load
- Medical-grade structure
- Avoid clutter and decoration

---

## 7. Logo Usage

Rules:
- Never stretch logo
- Always maintain circular integrity
- Use white version on dark backgrounds
- Use icon-only for app/favicon contexts

---

## 8. Key Principle

Everything must communicate:
“Precision care for movement restoration”

This is the emotional anchor of the brand.
